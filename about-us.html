  • CASA CLEANING SERVICES We make your home SPOTLESS.
Casa cleaning is a registered limited liability company that offers cleaning services for organizations, individuals and households. The company was set up by the CEO in view of her unrelenting passion for spotless, sparkling and well-organized environments. As expected, her passion for cleaning stemmed from a young age; while living with her parents, she learned how to keep the home clean and soon fell in love with staying in a clean and tidy environment. She later realized that a clean and tidy environment was a dream for many people and she decided to make that dream a reality by setting up a company that will provide top-notch cleaning services for all and sundry. In January 2022, Casa cleaning services was born as a dream come true after many years of research and investment in skills, tools and manpower that embodies the perfect blend of all that is needed to maintain a perpetual state of standard hygiene in offices, homes and public places. The unique services offered by the company has earned her a widespread recognition and clientele across many towns and cities. Today, Casa cleaning services stands as a beacon of excellence, integrity and innovation through its state-of-the-art equipment and workers who are highly trained to provide not just cleaning services but safety and security of properties within their work jurisdiction.

Frequently Asked Questions

At Casa Cleaning, we take pride in delivering top-tier cleaning solutions tailored to meet your unique needs. With a dedicated team of professionals, we ensure that your spaces are spotless, safe, and inviting.
What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We offer a range of cleaning services, including residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and post-construction cleaning. We also provide customized cleaning plans to suit your specific needs.
How do I book a cleaning appointment?
You can easily book an appointment through our contact us page. Just choose a date, time, and the type of cleaning service you need, and well take care of the rest.
Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?
Yes! We use environmentally friendly cleaning products that are safe for your home, your family, and the environment. However, if you prefer specific products, we are happy to accommodate your request.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No, you do not need to be home while we clean. As long as we have access to your space, our professional cleaners can complete the job, whether youre present or not. Well follow any instructions you leave with us to ensure everything is handled to your satisfaction.
How do you vet your cleaners?
All of our cleaners are thoroughly vetted, including background checks and in-depth training. We prioritize trust and professionalism, so you can feel confident that your home or business is in safe hands.
What happens if Im not satisfied with the cleaning?
Your satisfaction is our top priority. If youre not happy with the cleaning, contact us within 24 hours, and we will re-clean the affected areas at no additional charge.
Do you offer recurring cleaning services?
Yes, we offer flexible scheduling options for weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleaning services. Recurring customers also enjoy discounted rates.
